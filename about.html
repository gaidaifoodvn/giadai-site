About Us

Your Vietnamese food specialist

A privately owned import and export company, sourcing and supplying premium Vietnamese seafood and agricultural products for both domestic and international markets. We have built our name on three things: good price, good quality and good service.

Our Story

From the Mekong Delta to the world

Founded in 2002, Gia Dai has spent more than two decades sourcing and supplying Vietnamese seafood and agricultural products. We buy direct from trusted producers and ship to customers at home and overseas.

We work with green farms and fisheries where strict standards keep antibiotics and disease under control, so the products we ship are clean, safe and consistent. In 2012 we became a registered importer to the United States, opening the door to demanding international buyers.
